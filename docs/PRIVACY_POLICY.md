# Privacy Policy

**AVS PC Optimizer**  
**Effective Date:** 2026-07-23  
**Version:** 1.0.0

---

## Overview

Advanced Vision Software LLC ("we", "us", "our") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy describes how AVS PC Optimizer ("the application") handles your data.

## Information We Collect

### Currently Collected
- **System information:** Operating system version, hardware specifications, and performance metrics are collected locally for display purposes only.
- **Diagnostic logs:** Application logs are stored locally on your device for troubleshooting purposes.

### Not Collected
- **No personal data is transmitted** to external servers in this version.
- **No browsing history** is transmitted externally.
- **No telemetry** is sent in this version (telemetry is disabled by default).

### Future (With Consent)
- Anonymous usage statistics (opt-in)
- Crash reports (opt-in)
- Update check requests

## Data Storage

All data is stored locally on your device in:
- `%APPDATA%\@avs\pc-optimizer\` — Application data, logs, settings
- `%LOCALAPPDATA%\Programs\AVS PC Optimizer\` — Application installation

## Data Usage

Data collected by the application is used solely for:
- Displaying system information and health metrics
- Performing optimization tasks requested by the user
- Generating local diagnostic logs

## Third-Party Services

This version does not integrate with any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

## Data Retention

- Local logs are retained until manually deleted by the user.
- No remote data retention occurs in this version.

## Your Rights

- **Access:** You can view all data through the application's System Information page.
- **Delete:** You can delete logs and cached data through the application's settings.
- **Opt-out:** Telemetry and automatic updates are disabled by default.

## Children's Privacy

This application is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on our website and in future application versions.
